Gather some items and take outdoors or to the tv for some quick and easy paper clip charms.

I will try assembly without closing the paperclip itself so it still functions but these are wrapped.

Hang from tags or add another paper clip to the top for dangling off the edge of a book page.

Happy paper crafting!

A new charm for me, paperclips. So many options, use book pages, scraps, labels, tickets, words, ephemera or even fabric or lace. After assembly hang something from the lower loop as desired; beads, lace strips, ribbon or tassels. Quick and fun, great scrap buster!
