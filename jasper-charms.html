Jasper Charms

This charm project was inspired by jewelry, jasper stone earrings. An authentic junk recycle creation made with packaging material, magazine confetti and acrylic paint.

inspired by jewelry

What you'll need:

  • Packaging material
  • Torn magazine confetti
  • Gold acrylic or gel pen
  • Glue
  • Punches or scissors

I hope this inspires you to create some charms for your tags, journal page edges or spine embellishments. Great for boho or gypsy journals.

Happy paper crafting!
